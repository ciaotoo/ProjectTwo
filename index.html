About Milima

Elegant, minimalist and innovative activity and gps trackers.

Milima lies in the intersection between fashion, security and health industries. Our bracelets are uniquely designed to meet the needs of the active millennials, who are leaders in their industries, who want to maintain and active lifestyle and who want to feel secure. While, keeping their fashion game on edge.

Intended Users

Why Milima?

Milima is a combination of three words “Millimeter”, “Millennials” and “Telematics”. Millimeter reflects our commitment to attention to details in our designs. While, providing accurate GPS and activity tracking measures to our customers. Our products are designed by our interdisciplinary teams to create fashionable products that can easily be the go-to accessory to millennials while providing them with accurate gps and activity data.

Visual Elements

Logos / Icons

Milima’s logo is made of two main parts: logomark and word mark. The logo can be used with logomark and work mark, logo mark only and word mark only. The examples below shows these scenarios. The word mark is used at the top of this page.

Milima’s icons are very minimalist. All illustrations must be enclosed in an ellipse shape, in light green color. Those icons could be used in black and white as well.

Sample code:

<img src ="images/milima_logo.png" alt= "this should be milima's logo" width="50" height="50">

Details & Materials

Our bracelets have two main components. Stones and silver belts to connect the stones. We use fine 925 silver sourced ethically from Argentina and stones from all over the globe

All of our bracelets use ethically sourced stones. You can read our sourcing stories in the stories section. Our Microchips are enclosed inside those stones to insure durability. Our products can handle pressure and are waterproof.
